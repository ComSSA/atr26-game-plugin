Loot drops use all enabled weapons. Tag challenges with loot:easy, loot:medium, or loot:hard (and loot:off to disable). Each card rolls a rarity from the tier table, then a random weapon in that rarity. Easy never rolls legendary rarity; medium is stingy and cannot show two legendaries in the same pair; hard is the jackpot tier.
